The Letter For The King

The 3 Pieces

The Letter For The King - The 3 pieces is a companion game that we made during the minor Applied Game Design. This projects was made in 4 weeks with a group of four people, everyone having their own role.

Producer: Mette Duijnhouwer
Developer: Marc Heemskerk
Game Designer: Jeroen Bouwhuijs
Assets: Sylvia Haakman

Our goal in the game is that you need to find the 3 missing pieces. The 3 pieces are hidden in the castle of a dark mage. Its up to you to find the 3 pieces without getting caught by the ghostly knights. Every single piece has it's own puzzle to solve. When you got the 3 pieces you need to escape out of the castle, and give the 3 pieces to the Black knight with the white shield.
