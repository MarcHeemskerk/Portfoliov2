Tools of Choice

Unity

I always liked experimenting with JavaScript and other coding programs. It feels like puzzles I need to solve. I really like how I can combine my art and coding skills in Unity. During my minor Applied Game Design I really got to know Unity and C#. And now it's my favorite software combination and i'm planning to make a lot with it.

Sketch

I worked with Sketch since I did my internship at ZERO72 in 2015. That was the time when sketch was still in beta. I really love how this tool helps me to bring my imagination to the digital world.

Coding

The first time I ever touched code was back in 2013. I had with some friends a modded Modern Warfare 3 server. We wrote our own mods but honestly I didn't know what I was doing at the time. Much later in my first year of collage I started with HTML and CSS. After a year of HTML and CSS I discovered the power of JavaScript and right now I work a lot with C# for building Unity Games and making programs. I also make some fun little projects for my Arduino.

Adobe

The Adobe Suite is something I'm familiar with, it feels like i grow up along with it. With Photoshop I really loved the idea of being able to create and adjust any image in what I want. I love to make things in After Effects like fun little animations and putting things together. In Illustrator i can make vector illustrations. but for the most web/app assets I use Sketch. If I need to make a professional documents I use InDesign. All these skills put I to the test with my previous internship by NoProtocol.

Blender

Blender is a complicated but really fun tool to use. I had a few lessons in Blender during my Minor Applied Game Design and in my free time I like to experiment with it. I'm a big fan of low poly art and game assets.
